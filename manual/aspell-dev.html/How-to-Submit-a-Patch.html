2 How to Submit a Patch

Bug reports and patches should be submitted via the Sourceforge Tracker at http://sourceforge.net/tracker/?group_id=245 rather than being posted to any of the mailing lists.

The mailing lists are good if you need to check something out or need help or feedback from other readers, but they are not the best place to submit bugs or patches because they will likely get forgotten or lost within the mailing list traffic if not acted upon immediately.

Please make the effort to use the tracker.
